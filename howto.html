How to login

Upon accessing the web-page the first thing you will see is our registration form. In order to register you must select the food bank you are a part of and type in your email and password.
Once registration is complete you will have access to the login web-page. Type in your email and password in to this login form and you will have signed in!

How to Navigate

After succesfully logging in you can now navigate all pages freely. But where do you want to go? And what are you able to do? You have only 1 main option if you have not added anything to your inventory yet. That is the inventory button at the top of the webpage go ahead and click that. This is your inventory it is empty for now. Click on the transfers/request button. This will be where you ask other food banks for supplies.

How to Inventory

Once you are at the inventory you will be able to add items within a predefined list. Go ahead and add some test items! You do this by selecting the item bar and choosing an item and typing in the quanity of those items. You will see them appear in the inventory below. With the ability to delete or edit the quantity. Depending on how much you added you will see that it is either white, green, or RED! This is a indicator that you are either on medium stock, high stock or low stock. For red stock items you will see it in your little alert box in the bottom right corner.

How to Transfer/Request

Look at the top naviagation bar and click on transfer/request. From there you will see a form asking you for a foodbank which you will request items from, you will have the ability to leave a note and the amount you need. Once you hit submit the other foodbank will receive your request. They will be given 2 options approve or decline. Once approved it will go through one final confimation. On your side if you do not need the items anymore you will have the option to cancel the request.
